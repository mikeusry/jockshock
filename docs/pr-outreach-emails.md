## Response-handling cheatsheet

When a journalist says yes:

- Send the one-pager + 3 photos + Field Guide PDF within 1 business day
- Offer to put a sample in their hands (DTC ships to their address — works as a "review unit")
- If they ask for an exclusive embargo: yes, but cap at 7 days
- If they ask for a referral / second-source quote: connect them with Fred at D1 Training Athens (he's the most quotable for the gym-channel angle)

When a journalist says "send a press release":

- We don't send press releases. Send the one-pager and the Field Guide instead. Better artifacts to quote from, and reporters who only accept press releases aren't going to write a feature anyway.

When a journalist ghosts:

- 1 follow-up after 5 business days. Then drop. Don't be the founder who pings 4 times.
